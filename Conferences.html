Conferences

Mwananchi Hotel is an ideal venue for conferences, workshops and seminars whether involving a large or small group of participants. The hotel has state-of-the-art ultra modern conference facilities. Also secretariat and a well equipped business centre (with 24 hr internet services). The hotel has 5 conference halls which can accommodate between 50 - 300 persons. Break away and syndicate rooms are available for group discussion.
